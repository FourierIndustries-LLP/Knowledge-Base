Pull-up resistors and If/Else Statements (copy)

In this tutorial, we will learn about pull up resistors, and use push buttons to control lighting.

Pull-up resistors

Pull-up resistors are found in digital logic devices and microcontrollers.

When a pin in an electronic device like the SSTuino is configured as an input but there's nothing there, interference from nearby electronics may cause the device to read HIGH and LOW randomly. This effect is referred to as floating, and that's not what we want here.

To eliminate this, we will place a pull-up resistor to ensure that the pin is either in its HIGH or LOW state while using a little bit of current. Pull-up resistors are usually used on buttons and switches where there is a possibility of signal noise.
